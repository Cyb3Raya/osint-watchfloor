PRIVACY-POLICY

What this site does and does not do with your data. Short version: nothing is collected here.

What this site collects

Nothing. OSINT Watchfloor is a set of static pages. There are no accounts, logins, forms, comments, analytics, tracking scripts, or advertising. The site does not set cookies and does not build a profile of you.

Requests your browser makes to other companies

Loading this page causes your browser to contact a few outside servers. Those companies can see your IP address and basic request details, and handle that data under their own privacy policies:

  • Google Fonts - typefaces are loaded from fonts.googleapis.com and fonts.gstatic.com.
  • Article thumbnails - preview images are loaded directly from the publishers that host them, not copied onto this site.

This page sends a no-referrer policy with every request, so those servers are not told which page you came from.

Links to other sites

Every headline links to the publisher that wrote it. Once you click through, you have left this site and that publisher's privacy policy applies.

Where the content comes from

Headlines, summaries, and images are aggregated from public RSS feeds and remain the property of their original publishers. This site is not an official advisory service and does not modify or endorse what it aggregates - always confirm against the primary source.

Contact

Questions about this policy: aj.raya@proton.me

Changes to this policy

If the site changes in a way that affects privacy - adding analytics, comments, embeds, or other third-party services - this page will be updated to say so.
